Who we are...

The Center for Multi-Faceted Learning (CML) fosters ethical education and holistic development. Our program harnesses students' potential to excel in communication, problem-solving, and personal growth. CML offers diverse activities that broaden perspectives, encourages self-learning through MOOCs, and empowers students to discover their passions. Joining CML enriches both personal and intellectual growth, instilling optimism, constructive behavior, and confidence.
